Senior Structural Engineer

Dubai, UAE Senior Level Full-time

Overview

We're seeking an experienced Senior Structural Engineer to lead engineering teams on high-profile projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong portfolio of innovative structural engineering projects and expertise in complex structural systems.

Responsibilities

  • Lead structural engineering development for complex projects from design through construction supervision
  • Collaborate with clients, consultants, and internal teams to deliver exceptional engineering solutions
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to the consultancy's technical excellence
  • Prepare and present engineering proposals, calculations, drawings, and specifications
  • Ensure structural designs meet safety, code, performance, and budget requirements
  • Manage project timelines and coordinate with consultants and contractors

Requirements

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Civil/Structural Engineering
  • Minimum 8 years of professional experience in structural engineering
  • Professional engineering license (PE/Chartered Engineer) preferred
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating innovative structural solutions and technical expertise
  • Proficiency in structural analysis software (ETABS, SAP2000, STAAD Pro, Revit Structure)
  • Experience with high-rise buildings, complex structures, and BIM coordination
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and performance bonuses
  • Comprehensive health insurance
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Work on iconic, award-winning projects
  • Dynamic, collaborative work environment
